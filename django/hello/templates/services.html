How It Works

From field data to live dashboards

1
Data collected in the field

Healthcare workers record patient details, treatment outcomes, and disability assessments across all 12 states.

2
Processed & secured in the backend

Django handles data ingestion and Row Level Security ensures every user only accesses their authorized scope.

3
Visualized via Apache Superset

4
Decisions made faster

Program managers and state teams drill into their region instantly and act on live insights.
