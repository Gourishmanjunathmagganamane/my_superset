About LEPRA India

A leading public health organization working to eliminate leprosy and neglected tropical diseases across India through data-driven programs.

Who We Are

LEPRA India is a public health organization dedicated to fighting leprosy, lymphatic filariasis, and other neglected tropical diseases. We work at the grassroots level across 12 Indian states, ensuring that the most vulnerable communities receive quality healthcare and support.

Our Mission

To deliver quality healthcare, reduce disability, and restore dignity to people affected by leprosy and related conditions through community-based programs and data-informed decision making.
